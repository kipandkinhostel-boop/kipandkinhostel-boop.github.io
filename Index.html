About kip&Kin

36 beds total across 6 dorm rooms: three 4‑bed dorms (including one female dorm) and three 8‑bed dorms. Fully air‑conditioned, with curtains and wooden partitions so your bunk feels more like a little private space.

Chill spaces: hammocks to relax, a rooftop for sunbathing and yoga, and a basic CrossFit-style gym corner for a quick sweat.
